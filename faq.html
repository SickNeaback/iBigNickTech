FAQ

Answers to common questions about Big Nicks Tech.

About Big Nicks Tech

Who is Nicholas Seaback?
Nicholas Seaback is the founder and lead writer of Big Nicks Tech. I'm a passionate Apple enthusiast and tech writer dedicated to bringing you the latest news, reviews, and rumors about everything Apple.
When did Big Nicks Tech launch?
Big Nicks Tech launched in 2024 as a hobby project and has grown into a trusted source for Apple news and reviews.
Is Big Nicks Tech affiliated with Apple?
No, Big Nicks Tech is not affiliated with Apple Inc. in any way. All opinions expressed are my own and are not endorsed by Apple.
How do you get your information?
I gather information from a variety of sources including official Apple announcements, press releases, trusted industry sources, and carefully vetted rumors from reliable insiders.

Content & Coverage

What kind of content do you cover?
I cover news, reviews, rumors, and how-to guides for all Apple products including iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and more. I also cover Apple services and software updates.
How often do you publish new content?
I aim to publish new content daily, with major reviews and in-depth articles published as soon as products are available or news breaks.
Are your reviews unbiased?
Yes, all my reviews are based on hands-on testing and are completely unbiased. I don't accept payment for positive reviews and always give my honest opinion.
How accurate are your rumors?
I only report rumors from trusted sources with a track record of accuracy. However, rumors are by nature uncertain, so I always clearly label them as such and advise readers to take them with a grain of salt.

Contact & Support

How can I contact you?
You can reach me through the Contact page. I'm always happy to hear from readers, whether you have a question, tip, or just want to say hello.
Do you accept guest posts?
Currently, I'm not accepting guest posts, but I appreciate the interest! If you have a great story idea, feel free to reach out and let me know.
Can I submit a tip or rumor?
Absolutely! If you have information about upcoming Apple products or news, please send it to me through the Contact page. I always protect my sources.
How long does it take to respond to emails?
I receive a high volume of emails, so it may take a few days to respond. I appreciate your patience and will get back to you as soon as possible.

Technical Questions

What devices do you use for testing?
I use the latest Apple devices for all my reviews, including the newest iPhones, Macs, iPads, and Apple Watches. I believe in testing products in real-world scenarios to give you the most accurate assessment.
Do you cover non-Apple products?
Big Nicks Tech focuses exclusively on Apple products and services. However, I may occasionally cover products that work with Apple devices or compete directly with them.
Can I use your content on my site?
All content on Big Nicks Tech is copyrighted. If you'd like to use my content, please contact me first to request permission. I'm generally open to sharing as long as proper credit is given.
